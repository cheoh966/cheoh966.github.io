Gold undertones
This means your skin has more of an olive tint.
But you don't necessarily have to be tan!
If your skin is pale, but more ivory than pink,
you fall into this category as well.
A good test is to look at a visible vein.
If it appears green,
You probably fall into this category

Pink undertones
If your skin is rosier in complexion,
you probably belong in this group.
If you aren't sure, look at a visible vein.
If it appears blue-ish,
you probably fall into this category.
